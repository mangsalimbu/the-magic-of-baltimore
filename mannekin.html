Led Zeppelin in Baltimore

   Possibly the greatest live band to have ever graced the stage of clubs across America. These boys fearlessly performed with precision a host of the most difficult rock songs ever from bands such as RUSH, STYX, KANSAS, and EDGAR WINTER. "After years of touring and “bad decisions,” the band broke up in 1990, even as it was still packing venues. “We thought there wasn’t a future for us,” David VanLanding - the bands founder and lead singer.
